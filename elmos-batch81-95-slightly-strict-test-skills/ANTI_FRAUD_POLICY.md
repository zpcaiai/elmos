# Anti-Fraud Policy

The following invalidate certification:

- deleting, disabling or excluding a failing test;
- reducing assertion strength or increasing numeric/time tolerance without an approved semantic justification;
- replacing required mainframe, SAP, PLC, simulation, database, Salesforce, Apple, BEAM or OpenResty integration with a mock;
- changing requirements, business rules, safety boundaries or source fixtures to match generated output;
- hiding skips, quarantines, retries or unfavorable evidence;
- granting broader test-only privileges;
- allowing the generating Agent to independently certify its own Critical result;
- selectively sampling only records that agree in a parallel-run comparison.
