# Human Escalation Packet Builder
Read `../references/batch-8-repair-loop.md`. Include severity, source/target symbols/files/tests, UIR/effects/exceptions/async/obligations, normalized and raw evidence, attempted patches/outcomes, locked constraints, reproduction commands, decision options and risks.

Ask one concrete business or technical decision instead of requesting a repository-wide review. Assign P0/P1 owners and require the decision to update the Repair Manifest before re-entering automation.
