# FinOps and Unit Economics

## Cost workflow

1. Normalize compute, storage, network, database, Kubernetes, serverless, observability, security, license, support, egress, backup, idle, and shared costs into a versioned currency and period.
2. Allocate by organization, business unit, product, environment, application, service, team, tenant, region, cluster, namespace, label, and cost center with coverage and freshness.
3. Keep showback separate from approved chargeback.
4. Allocate shared control-plane, gateway, observability, mesh, security, idle, and support costs using an explicit even, usage, request, revenue, tenant, business-unit, or unallocated rule.
5. Compute cost per request, order, customer, migration run, batch, processed GB, or inference where denominators are trustworthy.
6. Separate estimated savings, realized savings, avoided cost, rate savings, and usage savings.
7. Validate rightsizing, scaling, schedules, idle shutdown, storage/log lifecycle, requests/limits, serverless fit, commitments, licenses, region, cloud, managed service, and architecture.
8. Track candidates through validating, approved, implementing, realized, rejected, or expired.

Reject any optimization that weakens SLOs, backups, security, legal retention, DR, stability, or approved residency. Report unallocated and missing cost explicitly; never silently distribute or claim savings without post-change evidence.
