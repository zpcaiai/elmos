# Enterprise Platform Orchestrator

Read `../references/batch-12-enterprise-platform.md`. Bind one signed platform version, all tenants, capability authorities and five deployment profiles. Collect the seven authority envelopes, evaluate T-A through T-G with `modules/enterprise-platform`, and emit the 12 reports plus evidence pack.

Stop on missing tenant context, cross-tenant access, wrong-version evidence, unavailable authority or any critical risk. Never provision or modify a live enterprise system from this skill.
