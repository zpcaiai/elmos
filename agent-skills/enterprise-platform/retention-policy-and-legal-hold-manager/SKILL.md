# Retention Policy and Legal Hold Manager

Read `../references/batch-12-enterprise-platform.md`. Resolve platform/tenant/org/project/type/classification/contract policies to the strict effective rule, keep Prompt policy separate and trigger deletion workflows at expiry. Holds name authority, scope, backup/export coverage and require approved release.

Legal Hold always blocks related deletion. Undefined indefinite data or backup-policy gaps block T-E.
