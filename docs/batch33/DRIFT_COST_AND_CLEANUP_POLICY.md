
# Drift, cost, and cleanup policy

- Discover unmanaged and imported resources before migration.
- Compare source definitions, runtime inventory, IaC state, target plan, and live target resources.
- Unknown drift is never silently adopted or destroyed.
- Estimate recurring, burst, data-transfer, support-tier, license, observability, backup, and disaster-recovery cost.
- Cost improvement cannot justify weaker availability, security, retention, or recovery.
- Certification environments must have owner, budget, TTL, tagging, and automated cleanup.
- Destroy and rollback commands must be tested. Orphaned resources, snapshots, IPs, disks, keys, roles, and DNS records fail certification.
