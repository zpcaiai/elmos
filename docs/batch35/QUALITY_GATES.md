# Batch 35 Quality Gates

Certification requires full P0 property, metamorphic, contract, data/money, security, query, numeric, representative-workload, counterexample replay, and assurance evidence required by the validation profile. Mutation score must meet the approved threshold; surviving critical mutants are zero. Critical fuzz crashes, forbidden concurrency outcomes, races/deadlocks/liveness violations, unresolved oracle conflicts, unsupported P0 claims, unknown required proofs, unreplayed counterexamples, and test-integrity violations are zero.

A technique may be conditional or blocked when unsupported. The pack must not claim universal proof from bounded search, finite testing, mutation score, or a single solver run.
