# Batch 34 quality gates

Certification requires at least 95% inventory, graph, work-unit, index, and source-map coverage; 100% idempotency, checkpoint recovery, runner placement, cache correctness, transfer resume, campaign, multi-PR, failure isolation, fairness, budget, control-tower freshness, all three scale benchmarks, disaster replay, and representative portfolio passes. Forecast interval coverage must be at least 80%. Zero-tolerance integrity, tenant, retry, budget, corruption, duplicate-effect, and test-integrity fields must be zero.
