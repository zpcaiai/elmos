# Certification Gates

A Batch 81–95 generated or modernized project is not certified by file generation alone. At minimum:

1. Source discovery coverage and dialect/version identification are complete.
2. Parsing and semantic indexing succeed or explicitly report unsupported constructs.
3. Business rules, data transformations, interfaces, jobs/workflows, and operational behavior remain traceable.
4. Generated target artifacts pass native parser/compiler/simulator/runtime validation.
5. Critical numeric, transaction, state-machine, timing, authorization, tenant, and safety semantics are independently tested.
6. Replay, retry, restart, cancellation, upgrade, and recovery behavior is validated where applicable.
7. User-owned artifacts are preserved.
8. Dependencies, templates, vendor packages, binaries, and evidence pass provenance checks.
9. Deployment or cutover has rollback or forward-recovery evidence.
10. Known limitations and unsupported constructs are included in the signed certification scope.
