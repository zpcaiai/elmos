## Safety boundary

Safety PLC, SIL-rated logic, emergency shutdown, and physical actuation require independent qualified engineering approval and hardware validation.

## Principal risks

- unsafe actuator command
- scan-cycle overrun
- interlock omission
- task-priority inversion
- I/O mismatch
- vendor semantic drift
